About me

Hello and welcome to my personal website! My name is Josiane Azar Chahwan, and I am a doctor specialised in Geography Curriculum at the Primary Education level. With a strong academic background, I hold a Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate degree from the Lebanese University.

Throughout my career for over than 20 years, I have had the privilege of teaching in various schools 3 different courses Geography, History and National Education, instructing diverse classes ranging from grade 4 to college level, besides being a geography coordinator.My teaching expertise allowed me to provide a holistic and comprehensive educational experience for my students.

Aside from my teaching responsibilities, I have actively contributed to the educational system by participating in the official exam corrections, ensuring fair and accurate assessments for students across the country. Additionally, my passion for sharing knowledge has led me to publish multiple academic articles and collaborate with publishing companies, contributing to the development of educational materials for schools.

Education is a powerful tool that can shape lives and create a better future, and I am committed to making a positive impact through my dedication to teaching and continuous pursuit of knowledge.
